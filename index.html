Eligibility

Who Can
Apply?

We welcome Papua New Guineans from all walks of life. Here's what you'll need.

Documents Required

  • Valid government-issued ID (Passport, Driver's Licence, NID)
  • 3 months recent pay slips or income evidence
  • 3 months bank statement (BSP, Westpac, ANZ, Kina)
  • Proof of residential address
  • Collateral documentation (for secured loans)

Basic Criteria

  • PNG citizen or permanent resident, aged 18–65
  • Employed, self-employed, or operating a registered business
  • Stable income for at least 6 months
  • No active default or bankruptcy proceedings
