How It Works

Get Funded in
3 Simple Steps

We've made the process straightforward so you can focus on what matters — your goals.

1
Apply

Visit any branch or apply online. Bring your ID, pay slips, and relevant documents. Our staff will guide you through the form.

2
Assessment

Our credit team reviews your application promptly. We look at affordability, not just credit history. Decisions within 24–48 hours.

3
Receive Funds

Once approved, funds are disbursed directly to your bank account or BSP account. Start achieving your goals right away.

Eligibility

Who Can
Apply?

We welcome Papua New Guineans from all walks of life. Here's what you'll need.

Documents Required

  • Valid government-issued ID (Passport, Driver's Licence, NID)
  • 3 months recent pay slips or income evidence
  • 3 months bank statement (BSP, Westpac, ANZ, Kina)
  • Proof of residential address
  • Collateral documentation (for secured loans)

Basic Criteria

  • PNG citizen or permanent resident, aged 18–65
  • Employed, self-employed, or operating a registered business
  • Stable income for at least 6 months
  • No active default or bankruptcy proceedings

Ready to Apply?

Visit your nearest branch or get in touch with our team. We'll walk you through the right product and have a decision for you quickly.
